Introduction


K – Hub is a center established in 2022, at Kakinada Institute of Engineering and Technology (KIET) in affiliation with International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT-H), as an extension to the KIET Global Coding Club (GCC) (established 2021). The main aim of the center is to nurture individuals in technology, through hands-on real world development experience.

Our collaborations

Raj Reddy Center for Technology and Society is to celebrate and amplify the passion of Prof Reddy in education and research for the bottom of the pyramid. The Center aspires to be a global hub to bring the latest research to solve grassroot challenges. The Center will take a bottom-up, problem-centred approach to leverage research and technologies in solving societal problems. It will identify a few socially relevant themes and take up projects in them to create a sequentially linked amplification of impact. It will take a wholesome view to the problem addressing all aspects, technology and otherwise, through to realising value on ground.

Achievements

I UDAYA


We are thrilled to announce that I. Udaya, an exceptional student from our K-HUB, was selected to represent her groundbreaking project at the prestigious nationwide conference, Leaders Connect, organized by Microsoft in New Delhi. This remarkable achievement was a testament to her remarkable talent and the transformative power of education. Udaya's journey began when she enrolled in a course program offered by Edunet, an esteemed educational platform. Fueled by her passion for innovation and guided by the invaluable knowledge gained through the program, she embarked on an extraordinary project that caught the attention of Microsoft. Her project stood as a testament to her ingenuity, perseverance, and dedication to making a meaningful impact in the world. The fact that Udaya's work was recognized at a national level spoke volumes about the quality and impact of her project. Representing our institution and her own extraordinary abilities, Udaya had the opportunity to showcase her project at the Leaders Connect conference. This platform provided a unique opportunity to network with industry leaders, professionals, and like-minded peers who shared a passion for driving positive change through technology and innovation.
